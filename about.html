About

The Hotel

Enjoy a Luxury Experience

LYF Hotels was founded in India in 2019 by D.Arun Kumar. Arun presented the LYF Hotel brand and business model to the founder of Paypay, Peter Thiel. Theil loved the idea and invested $100,000, jumpstarting Arun’s dream of building the number one hotel chain in the world.

LYF Hotels owns and operates chip urban resorts in the country offering superlative experiences and one boutique hotel offering superior experiences for corporate meetings and social gatherings with core Hospitality, Travel and Retail.

LYF Hotels is dedicated to guest service, attention to detail and an uncompromising commitment to quality. Over the years, the LYF Hotels brand has built a reputation for excellence in accommodation, food & beverage and service.

At LYF Hotels, our goal is to have people all over the world “Living The Good Life” and we pass down that same sentiment to our employees.

The high standards of quality – from the architecture to interiors, landscaping to facilities offered, impeccable service to delicious cuisines – are as discerning as guests have come to expect from it. Resorts under the LYF Hotels brand continuously strive for excellence to set new standards in the quality of their services and facilities.

LYF Hotels is using cutting-edge technology and top industry professionals to grow the India market and find new opportunities for expansion. Currently, we are investing in cloud kitchens, co-workspaces, extended stay residences, and multi-million dollar hotel projects.

LYF Hotels culture is built upon diversity, intelligence, and excitement. We believe that hard work truly does pay off, and those who show their value are rewarded. No position has a set plan for trajectory. Promotions have happened as fast as four months, and roles are often adapted to fit employee strengths.
